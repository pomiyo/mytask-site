Frequently Asked Questions

Is MyTask free?

Yes. MyTask is completely free to download and use.

Does MyTask require an account?

No. You can start using the app immediately without creating an account.

Does MyTask work offline?

Yes. Core features are designed to work without an Internet connection.

Where is my data stored?

Your tasks are stored locally on your Android device.

Will there be more features?

Yes. MyTask will continue to receive updates and improvements over time.
