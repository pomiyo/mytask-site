MyTask is a lightweight task manager designed for people who value speed, simplicity, and privacy. Capture ideas, attach photos, record voice notes, create reminders, and share events using QR-powered vCalendar—all while keeping your information stored locally on your device.

Why MyTask?

Designed to help you stay organized without sacrificing your privacy.

📱 Offline First

No account required. No cloud dependency. Your information stays on your device.

🖼 Image Attachments

Capture photos or choose multiple images directly from your gallery.

🎙 Audio Notes

Record voice notes and attach them to your tasks.

🎤 Speech-to-Text

Quickly dictate task titles and descriptions using Android's speech recognition service.

⏰ Smart Reminders

Never miss important tasks with reliable Android reminders and notifications.

📅 QR Calendar Sharing

Generate a QR code containing a standard vCalendar event that others can scan to preview and optionally save in their calendar app.
